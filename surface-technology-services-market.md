<p><p>Surface Technology Services encompass various processes to enhance material performance and durability. Anodising involves electrochemically treating metals to increase corrosion resistance. Ceramic coatings provide thermal protection and wear resistance. High Velocity Oxygen Fuel (HVOF) coating applies a dense metal layer for robust wear resistance. Ion implantation modifies surface properties at the atomic level. Plasma spray coats surfaces with a molten material, while combustion spraying involves applying melted materials to create protective layers. Other methods include various advanced coatings and treatments tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
