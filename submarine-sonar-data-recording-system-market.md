<p><p>The submarine sonar data recording system market is primarily segmented into passive and active sonar types. Passive sonar systems primarily listen for sounds emitted by submarines or other objects, making them ideal for stealthy detection without alerting targets. In contrast, active sonar systems emit sound waves and analyze the echoes returned from objects, providing more precise location and distance information. Both types are crucial for naval operations, surveillance, and threat detection, each catering to different tactical requirements in underwater environments.</p></p>
