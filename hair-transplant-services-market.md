<p><strong>The Hair Transplant Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair transplant services market primarily features two methodologies: Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUT involves removing a strip of scalp skin, from which follicles are extracted and transplanted, offering a high yield of grafts but leaving a linear scar. Conversely, FUE involves individually extracting hair follicles directly from the scalp, resulting in minimal scarring and quicker recovery. Both methods cater to various patient needs, influencing their popularity and market dynamics in hair restoration.</p></p>
